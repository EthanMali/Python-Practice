How to Play Snake Game

Instructions:

  1. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the direction of the snake.
  2. The snake will move continuously in the direction it is facing.
  3. Guide the snake to eat the food (displayed as a dot) that appears on the screen.
  4. Each time the snake eats food, it will grow longer.
  5. Avoid having the snake collide with itself or the walls of the game area.
  6. The game ends when the snake runs into itself or the boundaries.
